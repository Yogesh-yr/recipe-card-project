Classic Chocolate
Fudge Cake

❦

A rich, old-fashioned fudge cake finished with cream and strawberries — from bowl to table in half an hour.

Total Time 30 min
Serves 8
Difficulty Easy
30 MIN

Build progress 0 / 6 steps

Ingredients

  • 200 g dark chocolate, chopped
  • 180 g unsalted butter
  • 150 g caster sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 70 g plain flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 pinch sea salt
  • 100 g strawberries and whipped cream, to finish

Method

  1. 01Melt the chocolate and butter together in a heatproof bowl over simmering water, about 3 minutes.
  2. 02Whisk the sugar and eggs until pale and light, about 3 minutes.
  3. 03Fold the melted chocolate into the egg mixture in three additions.
  4. 04Sift in the flour and salt, folding gently until just combined.
  5. 05Pour into a greased tin and bake at 190°C for 16–18 minutes.
  6. 06Rest for 5 minutes, then top with strawberries and cream before serving.
